Our Story

The Dirt Busters LLC was founded by three friends—Jacob, Peter, and Erik—who shared a commitment to quality and a passion for flawless results. After years of bringing our high-rise commercial window washing expertise to businesses, we saw a need for the same level of precision and professionalism in the residential market. We started The Dirt Busters to bring professional-grade, streak-free cleaning to homes and businesses across south eastern Minnesota, one window at a time.

Our Commitment to You

At The Dirt Busters, our promise is simple: we will treat your home with the same care and respect we would our own. We are fully insured, committed to your satisfaction, and dedicated to delivering a spotless, streak-free result every single time.
